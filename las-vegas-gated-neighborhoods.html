Las Vegas Valley Gated and Guard-Gated Neighborhoods


There are many gated and guard-gated subdivisions and neighborhoods scattered about the Las Vegas Valley. Sizes range from a dozen or so homes on a couple of streets to large tracts of land with hundreds of homes. Many of the larger neighborhoods feature country-club style amenities such as tennis courts, community swimming pools, a clubhouse, walking trails, playgrounds as well as security patrols. Gate access can vary from key-pads to transponders to armed security guards stationed at the entrance. The focus here will be to concentrate on just those gated communities closer to downtown and the heart of Las Vegas â€“ generally between Buffalo to the West, Lamb to the East, Lake Mead Pkwy to the North and Russell to the South.
