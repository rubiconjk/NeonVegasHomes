Home is Possible Grant Program

The Nevada Home is Possible program provides a down payment grant along with a competitive fixed mortgage rate. This grant, which can total up to 4% of the loan's value, never needs to be repaid. It is free money to help the homebuyer achieve the dream of homeownership.

Key Benefits

 Provides a grant up to 4% of the home loan amount that can used for down payment assistance or closing costs

 Fixed interest rate 30-year loan

 No first-time homebuyer requirement

 Grant is non-repayable

 No asset limits for homebuyers

Program Requirements:

 Minimum credit score of 640 for government insured loans, 660 for manufactured homes and 680 for most conventional loans

 Qualifying income on mortgage application must be below $95,500

 Home price below $400,000

 Homebuyer education course required

 Must meet normal government or conventional loan underwriting requirements

 Homebuyer must live in home as primary residence

PrimeLending Neighborhood Closing Costs Assistance Program

National residential mortgage lender PrimeLending has announced the lauch of the Neighborhood Closing Cost Assistance Program. This is designed to promote homeownership through closing costs assistance. Eligible loan types are conforming, convential, FHA and USDA programs.

Qualified homebuyers may receive up to $1,500 in closing costs assistance based on either of the following criteria:

 Property must be located in a Low-to-Moderate Census Tract (LMIT); or

 Homebuyer's income is equal to or less than 80% of the Area Median income for the property location
