Spanish Oaks

Spanish Oaks is a green, tree-filled oasis near downtown Las Vegas. Located less than two miles west of the Las Vegas Strip, Spanish Oaks' centralized location makes it easily accessible from just about anywhere in the Valley. This is a guard-gated neighborhood with a 24 hour staff of armed-security guards. Amenities include 6 tennis courts (offering professional tennis lessons), 6 swimming pools, a putting green and award-winning landscaping. There is also a massive community clubhouse located atop the hill, offering spectacular views of the Las Vegas Strip.

Braewood Heritage

Braewood Heritage is a quite community located near E Desert Inn Rd and S Pecos Rd, just minutes east of the Las Vegas Strip. This gated community offers a clubhouse, three pools, a spa, tennis and basketball courts, playgrounds, a putting green and RV parking.

Sunrise Villas

The Sunrise Villas communities are centrally located in the Las Vegas Valley. All but Sunrise Villas 4 are gated. Built from 1974 to 1985, these neighborhoods offer tennis courts, swimming pools and a park-like atmosphere.

Quail Estates West

Quail Estates West is an age restricted (55+) community located less than 2 miles west of the Las Vegas Strip. This is a guard-gated neighborhood with mature trees, a pool, spa, clubhouse and covered parking.
